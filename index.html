Meenakshi did her MS in Innovation and Entrepreneurship from one of the most prestigious business schools in the world, HEC Paris. This program helped her develop crucial skills needed for both start-up enterprises and innovation-based business development in bigger firms.

Design Thinking (DT) is the process of bringing creative thinking to innovation that can be applied to any industry. It has brought great benefits in terms of value creation and competitive advantage to organizations. Meenakshi went to Stanford d.school in 2019 to learn DT along with educators from across the globe. She also has an in-depth training in DT as part of her master’s program from HEC Paris.

A passionate educator, she has a master’s degree in Education from IGNOU, with specialization in Education Management. Her deep interest in teaching & learning processes and educational psychology has steered her towards a social mission.

Intrigued by logical and analytical thinking skills, Meenakshi was attracted to computer science and chose it as her bachelor’s degree program. She enjoyed her stint as a programmer before finding her calling in education.
